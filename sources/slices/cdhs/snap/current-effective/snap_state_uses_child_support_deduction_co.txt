Colorado Code of Regulations, 10 CCR 2506-1, Supplemental Nutrition
Assistance Program (SNAP), effective 10/01/2023, section 4.407.5 Child Support
Expense Exclusion:

A household shall receive an exclusion from income for legally binding child
support payments made to or for non-household members.

The child support exclusion will be made from the household's total countable
gross income and prior to any gross income test to determine eligibility.

If the noncustodial parent makes child support payments to a third party
non-household member (e.g., a landlord, utility company, or health insurance
organization) in accordance with the support order, the payment shall be
included in the child support exclusion.

Model `snap_state_uses_child_support_deduction` as whether Colorado elects the
SNAP child-support deduction treatment under this current-effective state policy.
